Jack "Fluxare" Hytner Memorial Scholarship

This scholarship will be given to a student who lives out the mission that Jack was on: To show people that you can do anything, no matter your circumstances, and leave a positive influence on those around you.

Donate

We are taking donations from the community to help fund this scholarship and change students lives. 100% of proceeds will go towards this scholarship. Any funds over $500 will go towards the next year's scholarship fund, so that we can continue this scholarship for years to come. Thank you for supporting this cause as we seek to carry on Jack's legacy.
